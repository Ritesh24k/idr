About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on digital, cyber and technology risk roles.

Through collaboration with academic partners and industry leaders, IDR bridges the gap between theory and real-world risk management projects in high-consequence sectors.

Our Model

Academy

Training programs and bootcamps preparing students and professionals for digital risk careers.

Innovation & Incubation

Developing future risk models, AI governance frameworks and new intellectual property.

Advisory Services

Supporting organisations implementing risk frameworks like NIST, ISO 27001 and NIS2.

Our Community

IDR serves students, early-career professionals and practitioners working in regulated industries including financial services, government and critical infrastructure.

Our programs help individuals build expertise in digital, cyber and AI risk to meet the evolving needs of modern organisations.
